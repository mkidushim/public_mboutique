Welcome to MBoutique

We're a home based baking business that specialize in the making of French macarons, a gluten-free pastry item made from ground almonds. Our business began at the West Reading Farmers Market in 2011. Last year (2013) marked our third and final season of participation at the market. MBoutique was established to pay homage to the delicate French confectionery, the macaron. Our shop has been recognized as the connoisseurs of this delicious French pastry because of the wonderful variety of flavors from our great master chefs.

We love Macarons!

Renowned macarons, French delights of the moment ccan be met in a variety of flavors and colors and are brilliant precisely because because of their simplicity - a crispy coating, but delicate in a loose blanket jam, chocolate butter cream is spread inviting.

Macarons combines perfectly with champagne or white win, tea or hot chocolate, fresh juices and natural fruit flavored coffee and gaurantee that these little delights soon become friend that you cannot break.
